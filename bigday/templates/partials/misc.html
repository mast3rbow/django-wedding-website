Paperless

This is a paperless wedding. That means all of our save the dates, invitations, and thank-you notes are being delivered digitally. These small choices have helped us to enjoy a more eco-friendly wedding and save the trees. Thank you for your support and understanding!

Supporting our local community and enjoy good produce is core of our values. So we have choosen to only leverage local producers for our wedding.

Kandanga Farm raises organic beef using regenitive agricultural practices that are sustainable and more in line with nature. Local Bread is from a micro-bakery which frements their bread for 72 hours.
